sudeo classes are used to apply styles based on conditions also

those conditions are:

1.required(you cant submit the form without filling it)
2.optional(opposite to required (no need to mention in input tag))
3.readonly(only for reading purpose(just to watch)we cant fill the filed)
4.read-write(opposite to read-write (noneed to mention in input tag))
5.valid(it tells wheather the field valid or not based on (minlength,maxlength,pattern ..etc) )
6.disabled(used to buttons for making it action disabled)
7.default(to give styles for pseudo classes like checked,selected...etc with(+label))
8.in-range & out-of-range(to give styles of sudoclass values like min,max,...etc)
9.enabled(opposite for disabled (noneed to mention in input tag))
10.empty(this is to apply styles for which we had written nothing inside the tags)
11.not(to disselect the ids which we dont want to apply the styles)
12.first-child & last-child & nth-child nth-last-child (here we can give numbers & expressions to slect childs)(to give styles for first-childtag in a parent tag(for paragraph & div &li & ol...etc tags))
13.nth-last-type(),nth-of-type(),first-of-type,last-of-type(these are tags)
14.only-child (for thos having only one child)& only-of-type(for thos only another type of tag)
